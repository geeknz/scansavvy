Privacy Policy

Overview

ScanSavvy is an open-source barcode scanning and storage app designed with user privacy in mind. We do not collect, log, or store any personal information or data about our users. All data generated and stored by the app remains on the user's device.

No Data Collection

ScanSavvy does not collect any user data, including, but not limited to, device information, user information, or location data. The app does not require an internet connection to function, and it does not transmit any data over the internet.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
